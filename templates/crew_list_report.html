Document Categories
1. Personal Identification & Travel Documents
  • Valid Passport (with at least 6-12 months validity)
  • Seafarer's Identity Document (SID) or Continuous Discharge Certificate (CDC)
  • National Identity Card
  • Birth Certificate
  • Marriage Certificate (if applicable)
2. Medical & Health Documents
  • Medical Fitness Certificate (STCW Medical)
  • Vaccination Certificate / International Certificate
  • Dental Fitness Certificate
  • Eyeglass prescription (if applicable)
  • COVID-19 vaccination/test records (if required)
3. Employment & Contract Documents
  • Seafarer's Employment Agreement (SEA)
  • Letter of Appointment
  • Collective Bargaining Agreement (CBA) copy
  • Guarantee Letter from manning agency
  • Pay Slips
  • Income Tax documents
4. Travel & Port Entry Documents
  • Crew Visa for relevant countries
  • Transit Visa (if required)
  • Seaman's Book for visa stamps
  • On-signing/Off-signing travel details
Digital Copies: All documents should be maintained in secure cloud storage, personal email, USB drive, and mobile phone.
