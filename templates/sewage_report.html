MARPOL Annex IV Compliance Requirements
  • Sewage Management: All sewage must be either discharged to port reception facility, retained in holding tank, or discharged in designated areas ≥12 nautical miles from nearest land
  • Treatment Systems: Comminuted/disinfected sewage may be discharged ≥3 nautical miles from land; untreated sewage ≥12 nautical miles
  • GPS Position Tracking: Record precise latitude/longitude of discharge location for regulatory compliance
  • Tank Soundings: Document holding tank levels before and after all operations for accountability
  • Operation Logging: All discharge, transfer, or treatment operations must be recorded with start/stop times (UTC/GMT)
  • Port Reception Facility: Use PRF for port calls; transfer to holding tank if PRF unavailable
